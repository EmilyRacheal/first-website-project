About Us

Foundation for Depression Alleviation in Nigeria is a movement focused on drawing attention to mental health and the rise of suicide cases in Nigeria, with the aim of increasing attention and improving the call to action on mental awareness.

Through hands-on sensitization and targeted reorientation, FODAIN is driving the message of metal health not just across media houses and social media platforms, but also across educational institutions, organizations and government establishments.

Through volunteering and engagement of psychologists and other mental health experts, FODAIN is raising an army of mental health evangelists that will help change centuries of misconception and myths as regards mental health and emotional lapses.
